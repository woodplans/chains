1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Chains (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users access the Chains mobile application and related services.

Chains is a Web3-ready chat application for communities, creators, and users who want a modern chat experience with optional onchain identity and wallet connection.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Chains, we may collect or process the following types of information:

Data Type Examples Purpose
Firebase Anonymous UID An anonymous user identifier generated by Firebase Authentication. To create a basic guest identity, keep chat activity linked to your session, and secure access to app features.
Nickname / Chains ID Nickname, @username.chains, profile display name. To display your identity in chats, rooms, profiles, and community features.
Chat Messages Messages you send in public rooms, groups, channels, or direct messages. To deliver real-time chat, maintain conversation history, moderate abuse, and improve reliability.
Wallet Address Solana wallet address if you connect or verify a wallet. To support optional wallet identity, wallet recovery, identity verification, and Web3-related features.
Referral Code / Referrer Referral code, Play Install Referrer data, referral status. To track referrals, prevent abuse, and review reward eligibility.
Purchase / Subscription Status Google Play purchase status, active plan, product ID, subscription entitlement. To unlock Premium, Creator, Custom Chains ID, Room Credits, and Private Community Setup features.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide guest onboarding and account continuity through Firebase anonymous authentication.
  • To display nicknames, Chains IDs, room profiles, and community identity.
  • To deliver real-time chat messages and community interactions.
  • To support optional wallet connection, wallet recovery, and identity security.
  • To process referral tracking and prevent referral abuse or self-referral farming.
  • To validate Google Play purchases and subscriptions for premium digital features.
  • To maintain app safety, prevent spam, investigate abuse, and improve user experience.

4. Firebase Anonymous UID

Chains may use Firebase Authentication to create an anonymous UID when you start using the app. This allows you to access guest features without creating a traditional email account.

The anonymous UID may be linked to your chat activity, nickname, Chains ID, referrals, or premium status while you use the app.

5. Nickname and Chains ID

Users may have a nickname or Chains ID such as @username.chains. This identity may be visible to other users in rooms, messages, profiles, referral features, or community pages.

If you purchase Custom Chains ID Reservation, your purchase may grant an edit credit or identity-related entitlement inside the app.

6. Chat Messages

Messages sent in public rooms or community spaces may be visible to other users. Direct or private messages may be processed to deliver the message to the intended recipient.

Do not share sensitive personal information, passwords, private keys, seed phrases, or recovery phrases in chat messages.

7. Wallet Address and Web3 Identity

Wallet connection is optional. If you connect or verify a wallet, Chains may store your public wallet address and wallet status. We do not ask users to share seed phrases or private keys.

Wallet addresses are public blockchain identifiers. Depending on your wallet activity outside Chains, a wallet address may be linked to public blockchain transactions.

8. Referral Tracking

Chains may use referral codes, Play Install Referrer data, and referral status to understand whether a new installation came from a referral link.

Referral rewards are not automatically granted only because of an install. Referrals may be marked as pending and may require anti-abuse checks, such as preventing self-referrals, duplicate installs, suspicious activity, or referral farming.

9. Google Play Purchases and Subscriptions

Chains uses Google Play Billing to process digital products and subscriptions. This may include:

Chains Premium Monthly subscription that unlocks premium identity and room credit features.
Chains Creator Monthly subscription for creator and community-owner tools.
Custom Chains ID One-time purchase for a Chains ID edit/reservation credit.
Private Community Setup One-time premium setup entitlement for private community features.

We may store purchase entitlement status, active plan, product ID, and local credit status to unlock app features. Actual payment processing is handled by Google Play.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell users’ personal data. We may use trusted service providers to operate the app, including Firebase/Google services and Google Play Billing.

Data may also be disclosed when required by law, to enforce our terms, prevent abuse, investigate security issues, or protect users and the app.

11. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. Data transmitted between the app and backend services is intended to be encrypted in transit.

Users should never share private keys, seed phrases, or recovery phrases inside Chains. Chains only needs public wallet addresses for optional wallet identity features.

12. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Some public chat messages or community content may remain visible unless deleted or moderated according to app rules.
